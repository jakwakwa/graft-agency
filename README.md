# GRAFT.TODAY

## Overview

GRAFT.TODAY is a SaaS platform that helps small businesses and freelancers get more clients through automated lead generation and proposal management. 
                  
**For each of these businesses:**

1. The Graft Bot uses Google Search to find actual website URLs from scratch if not provided, it will then audit their online presence to determine if they are currently using visible AI technologies (like chatbots, AI agents). 

2. Graft Bot then identifies 2-3 potential pain points based on their industry and online presence, and drafts a highly personalised cold outreach email offering this value proposition: "${valueProposition}". The email should mention a specific pain point and how our solution helps. Keep it concise and professional.

3. Displaying the results in a list
- Company Name,
- Website Url,
- AI Presence,
- Pain Points,

4. After Audit Summary, Graft Bot will write draft email for you to review and approve before dispatch.

5. Prospecting Pipeline

**Set up your prospecting config**

**Add prospects via CSV or single row. Edit, delete, or process the queue manually.**

**Manage your prospect queue and review draft outreach before dispatch.**

**Run the pipeline from the Automation hub to generate results.**
